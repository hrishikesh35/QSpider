*** BOX MODEL ***

It is combination of "margin" and "padding".
 
    a. margin
        properties of margin
        1. margin-top
        2. margin-right
        3. margin-left
        4. margin-bottom

        Margin Properties are useed to move the container from current position.

    b. padding
        a. padding-top
        b. padding-bottom
        c. padding-right
        d. padding-left
            This properties are used to move the content inside the container
            when padding is used the container may deffer in size.
        
        e.box-sizing: border-box;
            this property makes the container to be in actual size (Height, width) which is given by the user.
